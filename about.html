About FEG-MISA

Empowering Ghana’s Medical Imaging Students

The Federation of Ghana Medical Imaging Students’ Associations (FEG-MISA) is the national body uniting radiography and medical imaging students across Ghana. We aim to foster leadership, professional growth, and collaboration among future imaging experts.

Through conferences, workshops, academic competitions, outreach programs, and research initiatives, FEG-MISA strengthens the capabilities and opportunities for all medical imaging students in Ghana.
