About FEG-MISA

Strengthening Medical Imaging Education in Ghana

The Federation of Ghana Medical Imaging Students’ Associations (FEG-MISA) is the national coordinating body for all Medical Imaging/Radiography student associations across Ghana. Our mission is to unite, empower, and develop the next generation of imaging professionals.

Through conferences, workshops, outreach programs, research development and academic competitions, we foster excellence, leadership, and collaboration among students from all medical imaging institutions nationwide.
